Audi - together forever...
a German automotive company within the Volkswagen Group, specializing in the production of cars under the Audi brand. The headquarters is located in the city of Ingolstadt (Germany). The motto is Vorsprung durch Technik (from German - "Progress through technology"). The volume of production in 2016 was about 1,903,259 vehicles. Audi in its current form is the heir to the Auto Union concern, formed as a result of the merger of four companies that produced cars and motorcycles under the brands DKW, Horch, Audi and Wanderer. As a result of the transformation, only the Audi trademark was retained.
